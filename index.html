CubicNurika Privacy Policy

Last updated:

1. Data Collection

Our app does NOT collect personal information like your name, email, or contacts. However, we use:

  • Huawei Ads Kit: Collects advertising ID, IP address, and device info (model, OS) for ad targeting
  • Game Analytics: Anonymous data about gameplay (level progress, session length)

2. Why We Collect Data

  • To show relevant ads and support free gameplay
  • Improve game performance and fix bugs
  • Understand player preferences (no personal identification)

3. Data Sharing

Data is shared only with:

  • Huawei Ads partners (for ad delivery)
  • Analytics services (in aggregated form)

We never sell your data to third parties.

4. Your Rights

  • Opt-out of personalized ads in device settings
  • Request data deletion via email
  • Disable analytics in game settings
