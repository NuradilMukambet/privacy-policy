CubicNurika Privacy Policy

Last updated:

⚠️ Key Summary: We use Unity Ads which collects device data for personalized ads. No emails/names are collected. See details.

1. Advertising and Analytics

Our app uses:

  • Unity Monetization:
    • Collects Advertising ID (AAID/IDFA)
    • Device data (model, OS version, language)
    • IP address (for approximate location)
    • Ad engagement metrics
  • Unity Analytics (anonymous):
    • Gameplay sessions duration
    • Level progression
    • Crash reports

This data is used solely for ad targeting and game improvement.

2. Data NOT Collected

We do NOT collect:

  • Your name or email address
  • Phone contacts or messages
  • Exact GPS location
  • Payment information

3. Your Control Options

  • Opt-out of personalized ads:
    • Android: Google Settings → Ads → Opt out
    • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising → Limit Ad Tracking
  • Reset Advertising ID in device settings
  • Delete analytics data: Contact us via email below

4. Third-Party Partners

Data may be shared with:

  • Unity Technologies (Privacy Policy)
  • Ad networks via Unity's mediation
